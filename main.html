EOS Matrix and Feitiansuye investing in Begej-Plast

EOS Matrix and Chinese company "Feitiansuye", signed an agreement on selling of company Begej-Plast from Perlez. The contract was signed by the director of the Chinese company Haiou Liu and the Deputy Director General of EOS Matrix Jelena Jovic, in the presence of Mayor of Zrenjanin Čedomir Janjić. The Chinese investor has committed to invest half a million euros of which EUR 180,000 will be invested in the factory, and about 350,000 euros in to machines. This investment will provide 100 new jobs in the region.
